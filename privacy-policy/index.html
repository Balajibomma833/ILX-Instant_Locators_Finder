1. Introduction

Instant Locators Finder (ILX) is a Chrome extension developed by Balaji Bomma. It helps QA engineers and testers identify visible and actionable UI elements on webpages and export their locator information for automation purposes.

2. Information We Collect

ILX does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). ILX does not access or process:

  • Personal data
  • Sensitive information
  • Login or authentication data
  • Payment or financial details
  • Browsing history
  • User identity or profile information

3. What ILX Reads

ILX only analyzes the DOM of the active webpage to extract automation-related details such as:

  • Relative XPath
  • Element attributes
  • HTML tag type (button, link, input, etc.)
  • Visible text/labels of actionable elements
  • Accessibility attributes (if visible)
